Featured Research Project

Artificial Intelligence and Ethics

This project focuses on ethical implications of artificial intelligence, aiming to develop guidelines for responsible AI development and use.

Artificial intelligence (AI) empowers machines to learn, adapt, and solve complex problems, transforming industries and enhancing human capabilities worldwide.

As AI evolves, it holds vast potential to reshape society, raising ethical questions and challenges around privacy, fairness, and employment that demand thoughtful attention and responsible development.
