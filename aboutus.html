About Us

About Sherubtse College

Sherubtse College, located in Kanglung, Trashigang, Bhutan, is one of the premier institutions of higher learning in the country. Established in 1966, the college has a rich history of academic excellence and a commitment to providing quality education to its students. With a diverse range of programs and a vibrant campus life, Sherubtse College is dedicated to nurturing the intellectual and personal growth of its students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and ethical values. We aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are prepared to contribute positively to society and the world.

Our History

Sherubtse College was established in 1966 with the vision of providing quality higher education in Bhutan. Over the years, the college has grown and evolved, offering a wide range of programs and becoming a center of academic excellence in the region.
