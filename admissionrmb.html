Admissions Information

At Sherubtse College, we offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to help you achieve your academic and career goals. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that all prospective students have the opportunity to join our vibrant academic community.

Undergraduate Admissions

Our undergraduate programs are designed to provide a strong foundation in various fields of study. We offer a diverse range of majors and minors, allowing students to tailor their education to their interests and career aspirations.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Arts in English
  • Bachelor of Business Administration
  • Environmental Political Science

To apply for our undergraduate programs, please visit our admissions portal and complete the online application form. You will need to submit your academic transcripts, a personal statement, and letters of recommendation.

Postgraduate Admissions

Our postgraduate programs are designed for students who wish to further their education and specialize in their chosen field. We offer a range of master's and doctoral programs that provide advanced knowledge and research opportunities.

  • Master of Science in Data Science
  • Master of Arts in Literature
  • Master of Business Administration
  • PhD in Environmental Studies

To apply for our postgraduate programs, please visit our admissions portal and complete the online application form. You will need to submit your academic transcripts, a research proposal, and letters of recommendation.

Admissions Process

Step 1: Application

Complete the online application form and submit all required documents, including academic transcripts, personal statement, and letters of recommendation.

Step 2: Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with our admissions committee to discuss their academic background and career goals.

Step 3: Admission Decision

Successful candidates will receive an offer of admission, along with information on how to accept the offer and enroll in the program.
